# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant**, version 2.1.

The full text is maintained upstream at:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

By participating in this project — opening issues, submitting pull
requests, commenting, or any other form of engagement — you agree to
abide by it.

## Reporting

Concerns about a contributor's conduct should be raised privately with
the maintainers. See the contact details in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md)
or the `author` field in [`package.json`](./package.json).

Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers will respond within a
reasonable timeframe and may take action up to and including a permanent
ban from project spaces, per the enforcement guidelines in the
Contributor Covenant.
